Making Moves
  Cursor is moved to the cell touched.
  Slide(Drag) moves cursor to the direction.
  Touch on the cursor position makes a Move.
  Yellow icon button displaying coordinate below the board
  also makes a Move to the cursor positon.
  One touch mode is a Ajagoc option.
  When one touch mode, 1st touch without sliding makes a move.
