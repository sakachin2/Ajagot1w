Wi-Fi Direct:(From Android 4)    
    Open dialog "Wi-Fi Direct",
    which follows operations Discover,Pair and Connect.
Bluetooth:
    Open dialog "Bluetooth Connection",
    which follows operations Pair(Discoverable and Scan)
    and make connection(Accept and Connect)

NFC-WD:(Available for the device attached NFC tag)
    Open dislog "NFC : WiFi Direct".
    Close NFC tag part of the two devices
    displaying this dialog.
    Touch the screen of one device
    when "Touch to beam" is shown.
    Two device will be connected
NFC-BT:(Available for the device attached NFC tag)
    Open dislog "NFC : Bluetooth Insecure".
    Close NFC tag part of the two devices
    displaying this dialog.
    Touch the screen of one device
    when "Touch to beam" is shown.
    Two device will be connected with InSecure mode.

Connect:
    Connect to selected Server or Partner.
Edit:
    Edit the selected destination.
Add:
    Add new destination to the list.
Delete:
    Remove the selected entry.
UnDelete:
    Undo the previous Delete.
Upwards:
    Move upwards the selected line.
Downwards:
    Move downwards the selected line.
 
(Forllowings are for "Partners connection")

Open?: (Use on LAN) 
    Search opened partner of the partners list.
    List the partner with status of "Start Server"
    (Top menu --> Partner --> Start Server).
    You can connect from the panel "Open Partners".
    Wireless LAN environment is required 
    to search opened partner.
